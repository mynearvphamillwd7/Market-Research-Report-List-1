<p><p>Radiant heating panels are designed to efficiently transfer heat to spaces through two primary types: electric radiation and infrared radiation. Electric radiation panels use electrical energy to generate heat, warming the surrounding air and surfaces. In contrast, infrared radiation panels emit infrared rays that directly warm objects and people in the room, providing a more immediate and comfortable heat experience. Both types are popular for their energy efficiency and ability to maintain consistent temperatures in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
