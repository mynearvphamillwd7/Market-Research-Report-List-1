<p><p>The edge bander market is primarily divided into two types: single-sided and double-sided edge banders. Single-sided edge banders are designed for applying veneer or laminate to one edge of a workpiece, making them ideal for smaller production needs or simpler projects. In contrast, double-sided edge banders can apply material to both edges simultaneously, increasing efficiency and productivity for larger operations. Both types cater to various industries, including furniture manufacturing and cabinetry, adapting to different operational scales and requirements.</p></p>
