<p><p>The Chip-on-Board (CoB) LED market includes three primary types: Lateral Chip, Vertical Chip, and Flip Chip. </p><p>Lateral Chips are oriented horizontally, enabling efficient thermal management and enhanced light output. Vertical Chips, on the other hand, are mounted vertically for improved electrical performance and compact designs. Flip Chips flip the die for better light extraction and minimized thermal resistance. Each type caters to specific applications, balancing performance, efficiency, and manufacturing costs in the evolving LED landscape.</p></p>
