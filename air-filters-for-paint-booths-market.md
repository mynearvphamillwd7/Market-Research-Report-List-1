<p><p>Air filters for paint booths are crucial for maintaining a clean and safe working environment by capturing airborne particles and contaminants. Coarse filters are designed to trap larger particles, such as dust and lint, protecting more sensitive filters downstream. HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Air) and ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters provide higher filtration efficiency, capturing smaller particles down to 0.3 microns or even 0.1 microns, ensuring optimal air quality and preventing hazardous pollutants from affecting paint finishes.</p></p>
