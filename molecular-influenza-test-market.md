<p><p>The molecular influenza test market includes several key types: </p><p>- **RT-PCR (Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction)**: This highly sensitive method detects the virus's RNA, providing accurate results quickly. </p><p>- **TMA (Transcription-Mediated Amplification)**: A nucleic acid amplification technique that amplifies RNA for detection.</p><p>- **LAMP (Loop-Mediated Isothermal Amplification)**: A rapid test that amplifies DNA in a single step at a constant temperature.</p><p>- **NASBA (Nucleic Acid Sequence-Based Amplification)**: It specifically targets RNA for amplification and detection.</p><p>- **Other methods** include various novel techniques that may emerge in the market.</p></p>
