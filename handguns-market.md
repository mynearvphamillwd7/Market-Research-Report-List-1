<p><p>The handgun market is categorized into three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Automatic handguns fire continuously when the trigger is held down, allowing for rapid fire. Semi-automatic models fire one round per trigger pull and automatically reload the chamber, offering a balance of speed and control. Manual handguns, often revolvers, require the shooter to manually prepare each shot, providing simplicity and reliability.
